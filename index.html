About SASE

The Society of Asian Scientists and Engineers,or SASE for short, is a professional organization that promotes the advancement of Asian heritage scientists and engineers in education and employment, as well as celebrating diversity and providing opportunities to volunteer and make contributions to local communities.

SASE is a national organization that was founded in November 2007 to help Asian heritage scientific and engineering professionals achieve their full potential and to provide the first organization similar to NSBE and SHPE where students representing all of the pan Asian cultures could connect and support each other.

SASE's mission is to:
"Prepare Asian heritage scientists and engineers for success in the global business world."
"Celebrate diversity on campuses and in the workplace."
"Provide opportunities for members to make contributions to their local communities."

SASE membership is open to men and women of all ethnic backgrounds.
